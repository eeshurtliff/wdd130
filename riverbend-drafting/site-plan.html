Home page

A Personalized Touch Riverbend Drafting and Design serves Idaho Falls and surrounding areas. At River Bend Drafting and Design, we specialize in personalized custom homes that meet your needs, budget, and timeframe. It can be frustrating looking to build a custom home and feeling as if you can't find a plan that feels right for you or your family. Or searching through hundreds of plans online, only to feel nothing fits all of your wants or needs. We believe your home should reflect the needs of your family now and in the future. We specialize in taking your list of wants and needs and creating the perfect plan. All at a reasonable price and timeframe. About Riverbend Drafting and Design River Bend Drafting and Design specializes in personalized custom homes that meet your needs, budget, and timeframe. River Bend Drafting and Design was started in 2017 to create extraordinary housing projects that reflect the needs, desires, and personality of each homeowner. Integral to providing quality service is maintaining attention to detail, utilizing 3D computer technology, incorporating specific requirements from the client, and treating each client as a person and not a number. River Bend Drafting and Design believes the perfect home comes in many sizes and shapes; your perfect home can be a brand-new design or an old house remodel; your perfect home could be grand, or minimal. What matters is your house fits you, and we want to help you get there. Our mission at River Bend Drafting and Design is simple: to provide high-quality services in a timely manner. Our team caters to each project’s specific needs to ensure excellence. We hope you’ll find what you’re looking for. For more information or general inquiries, feel free to get in touch today. Riverbend Drafting and Design’s Head designer is Elise Shurtliff.

New Home Design

New Home Design Here are the steps we will take to help you design your new home: Steps to the process: 1. Make a list of required features and things that would be nice to have OR Find plans you want to model or plan after. 2. Have a size estimate you want to keep your house at. 3. Meet with Elise to talk about your plans and get started. A $500 deposit is required and will be applied to the final balance when you receive finished plans. 4. We will send you a draft in a few days for you to look over and make changes to. - Changes are good! The goal is to create the best home for you, let us know what you really want. - This might take a few iterations, this is okay, and I promise we will get there. 5. Finish the plans and create a site plan if you have your lot. 6. Send Plans to multiple builders for bids. 7. If you have changes after talking to builders that is okay, I am happy to help you make changes (included in the original price, unless significant changes are made). 8. Send your plans to an engineer if needed, get a permit, and start building.

Remodel and 3D renderings

Remodel If you want to do a remodel on your home, our designer will come to your home for a personal consultation on where you are, where you want to be, and how we can help you get there. Remodels Include 3D renderings on finished product. There is a $250 deposit required at beginning to go toward the final bill. Charge is $50 per hour with a 10-hour minimum. 3D Renderings If you are a designer or drafter and would like help with your renderings, we are happy to help you present your plan to the world. A $250 deposit is required to be put toward the final bill; $50 per hour is charged for renderings created.
