About Us

River Bend Drafting and Design specializes in personalized custom homes that meet your needs, budget, and timeframe. River Bend Drafting and Design was started in 2017 to create extraordinary housing projects that reflect the needs, desires, and personality of each homeowner. Integral to providing quality service is maintaining attention to detail, utilizing 3D computer technology, incorporating specific requirements from the client, and treating each client as a person and not a number. River Bend Drafting and Design believes the perfect home comes in many sizes and shapes; your perfect home can be a brand-new design or an old house remodel; your perfect home could be grand, or minimal. What matters is your house fits you, and we want to help you get there. Our mission at River Bend Drafting and Design is simple: to provide high-quality services in a timely manner. Our team caters to each project’s specific needs to ensure excellence. We hope you’ll find what you’re looking for. For more information or general inquiries, feel free to get in touch today.
